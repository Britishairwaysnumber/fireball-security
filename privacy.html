Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Fireball Cyber Labs ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Fireball Endpoint Protection software ("Software"). Unlike other security companies, we do not sell your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to verify your license and provide security updates:

  • Account Data: Name and Email Address (provided during purchase).
  • Device Data: A unique hardware identifier (to enforce the 3-device limit).
  • License Data: Your License Key and activation status.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

To ensure your privacy, the following data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • We do NOT collect your browsing history.
  • We do NOT collect the contents of your files.
  • We do NOT collect your passwords or banking details.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your contact information solely to:

  • Send you your License Key after purchase.
  • Notify you of critical security updates or subscription expirations.
  • Respond to your customer support requests.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers for specific infrastructure needs:

  • Supabase: For secure database management of license keys.
  • Stripe/PayPal: For processing payments. We never see your credit card number.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@fireballsecurity.com.
